Hardware Identification Chart

By Harrison Procter

Introduction

There are many parts in a machine that make the machine work. One of them is the motherboard, which is the main part for all machines (give or take a few). There is also the graphics card, which is responsible for, well it is in the title (graphics). There is also the power supply that supplies power to the machine. These parts of the computer will be displayed in this presentation.

Mother Board

The motherboard is responsible for holding some of the most important parts of the computer such as the CPU and memory connectors.
Titanx Graphics Card This part of the computer is responsible for texturing images generated inside of the computer.
PCI Card This part of the computer is used to attach hardware to the computer.
Hard Drive The hard drive stores all of the computers data and memory, a better hard drive means more storage and faster speed.
Power Source The power source is responsible for supplying power to the computer.
